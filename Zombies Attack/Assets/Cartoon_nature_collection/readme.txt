This package contains 97 different cartoon nature props like 
trees, palms, flowers, rocks etc to place them in your scenes.
Some potted plants for indoor scenes are also included.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that contains all the prefabs.

Most of the objects come with predefined colliders.
For most of the objects several texturesets can be find in the package.

Textures-sizes between 64x64 to 1024x1024
